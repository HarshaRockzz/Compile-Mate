About CompileMate

CompileMate is a gamified competitive programming platform designed to make learning algorithms and data structures fun, social, and rewarding.

Our Mission

To empower programmers of all levels to improve their skills, compete globally, and enjoy the journey of problem solving through engaging contests, rewards, and community features.

Key Features

  • Vast library of curated problems
  • Real-time contests and leaderboards
  • MateCoins and gamified rewards
  • AI-powered code review (coming soon!)
  • Marketplace for digital goods
  • Community hints and discussions
